Tree-Plenish

Since November 2020, I've been working as the director of technology of Tree-Plenish, a nationwide non-profit sponsoring tree-planting events at high schools across the country.

Tree-Plenish is uniquely structured: we handle the logistics, supply, and mentorship while local student leaders are responsible for the marketing and execution of their event.
