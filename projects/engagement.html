Engagement Tracking

Using URL redirection and customized link tracking to ethically source data to analyze the efficacy of outreach campaigns and general communications.

General Ad Tracking

Over the summer our engagement tracking system was used to measure the success of four seperate online advertisement campaigns. These allowed us to track nuanced differences between ad content, platform, and timing while also verifying campaign execution.


Email Tracking

To help validate and analyze our email outreach efforts, we embed a redirect link which logs the engagement. This does not collect any individual data on the user and the link is preset for privacy*. While vague, the resulting data has helped immensely in planning the final stages of our outreach strategy.


*Note, Tree-Plenish does not collect any unauthorized user data and any engagement data used for large scale analytics is impersonal and automatically deleted after a month.
