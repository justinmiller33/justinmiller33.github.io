The Scissor Lift

4-Report, Cumulative Project for Mechanical Design (ME 4550).



Design Proposal Report:

Proposal with initial lift design and use cases.


Component Design and Analysis Report:

Lift Design broken down by components with comprehensive analysis on static and fatigue failure modes.


Connection Design and Analysis Report:

Connection Design with failure analysis on all welds and bolt configurations.


Power Transmission Design and Analysis Report:

Power Screw and gear train design with failure analysis.
